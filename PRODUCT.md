# Product Summary

Andrea Milan's website is a single-page editorial profile. It should feel authored, minimal, and memorable, with the tone of a contemporary personal biography rather than a product homepage.

## Audience
- Hiring leaders and recruiters evaluating product leadership range
- Founders, operators, and peers looking for taste and strategic clarity
- Readers who want a small window into Andrea's product thinking
- Professional contacts looking for a safe public way to connect

## User Goals
- Understand who Andrea is and what kind of product leader he is
- Read the career story quickly without scanning a full CV dump
- See a few selected pieces of writing
- Reach Andrea through public channels without friction

## Product Principles
- Editorial over modular
- Personal over corporate
- Curated over exhaustive
- Dark, spacious, and typography-led
- Calm and restrained motion
- Low reading effort, especially on mobile
- Simple information architecture

## Information Architecture
- Intro
- Experience
- Education
- How I think
- Connect

## V1 Constraints
- Fully static
- GitHub Pages compatible
- No backend
- No contact form
- No separate writing/about/contact pages
- Minimal client-side JavaScript
- Medium represented lightly as a secondary link rather than a primary content block

## Hosting Implications
- Static export only
- No runtime server dependencies
- No middleware or API routes
- All content is local and build-safe

## Future Directions
- Further motion tuning if it remains lightweight
- Optional sanitized CV download
- Selected case studies if they fit the same single-page narrative
