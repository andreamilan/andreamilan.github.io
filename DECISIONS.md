# Major Decisions

## 2026-03-14
- Chose a design-first approach where visual quality and hierarchy come before advanced functionality.
- Chose a curated narrative instead of a full CV dump, while still including every professional experience and education entry.
- Chose a static-first architecture built for GitHub Pages rather than runtime hosting features.
- Chose Material Design 3 as a systems foundation, interpreted through a more editorial and personal visual language.
- Chose not to publish Andrea's email address or provide an email contact form.
- Chose to omit the downloadable CV in v1 because the provided PDF exposes Andrea's email address.

## 2026-03-14 Redesign
- Replaced the multi-page structure with a single-page anchored architecture.
- Switched the visual direction to dark mode by default.
- Replaced feature-card repetition with a narrative layout and an editorial timeline.
- Folded About into a Principles section and Contact into a lightweight Connect section.
- Reduced CTA density to a small number of intentional links.
- Rewrote the visible site copy in first person.
- Replaced the old article list with a lightweight Medium mention and link.
- Added restrained motion polish through reveals, active nav feedback, and very soft ambient background movement.
- Pulled Education out into its own section for a cleaner and more legible page structure.
- Removed the standalone Writing section so Medium remains a subtle secondary signal rather than a primary content block.
